### Hi, I'm Samuel! 👋

My pronouns are he/him/his. I am a second year graduate student at the University of Michigan School of Information with a concentration in Digital Archives, Library Science, and Preservation. My current research interests include digital preservation, public libraries, equitable content moderation, transness and digital self-representation, trans community folk theories, and information practices. I want to learn how trans communities use social media to explore identity and provide resources and support networks for one another. I have a BA in Social Relations and Policy from James Madison College at Michigan State University. Prior to my graduate program, I worked at both public libaries and government documents collections.

📖 **Currently learning about:** Folk theories developed by marginalized social media user communities
