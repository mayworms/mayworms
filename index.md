### Hi, I'm Samuel! 👋

My pronouns are he/him/his. I am a second year graduate student at the University of Michigan School of Information with a concentration in Digital Archives, Library Science, and Preservation. My current research interests include digital preservation, digital literacy, equitable content moderation, marginalization and digital self-representation, and information practices. I received my BA in Social Relations and Policy from James Madison College at Michigan State University. Prior to my graduate program, I worked in both public and academic libraries. 

✍️ **Currently researching:** Online digital literacy resources for marginalized user communities 👀 

📖 **Previously researched:** Marginalized social media user communities, online content moderation, folk theories
